LCD:
Pin 1 to GND
Pin 2 to 5V
Pin 3 to wiper
Pin 4 to Arduino pin 12/6/A4
Pin 5 to GND
Pin 6 to Arduino pin 11/7/A5
Pin 11 to Arduino pin 5/A3
Pin 12 to pin 4/A2
Pin 13 to pin 3/A1
Pin 14 to pin 2/A0


Keypad:
Keypin = Arduino Pin
C0 3   = 8
C1 1   = 7
C2 5   = 6

R0 2   = 5
R1 7   = 4
R2 6   = 3
R3 4   = 2
